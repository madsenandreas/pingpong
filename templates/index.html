Available Game Modes

  • Best of 1 Set:

    A single set played to 11 points. Perfect for quick games.

  • Best of 3 Sets (Default):

    Each set is played to 11 points. The player or team that wins 2 out of 3 sets wins the match.

  • Custom Mode:

    Configure custom rules like the number of sets or points to win.

Game Rules

  • Scoring:
    • Games are typically played to 11 points.
    • A player must win by at least 2 points.
    • Matches usually consist of best-of-3 sets.
  • Serve:
    • Each player serves two consecutive points, alternating serves.
    • The serve must start with the ball resting on the palm, tossed at least 16 cm upward.
    • The ball must bounce on both sides of the table.
  • Gameplay:
    • Players take turns hitting the ball after it bounces once on their side.
    • A point ends if the ball bounces twice, is hit into the net, or is hit off the table without touching it.
  • Doubles:
    • Partners alternate hits during rallies.
    • The serve must go diagonally across the table.
  • Let:
    • A serve is replayed if it touches the net but lands correctly on the opponentâ€™s side.
  • Winning:
    • The first player to 11 points (with a 2-point lead) wins the game.
    • The match winner is the first to win the majority of games (e.g., 2 out of 3 in "Best of 3").
